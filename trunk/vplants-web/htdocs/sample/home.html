vPlants: a Virtual Herbarium of the Chicago Region.

The online resource for Plants and Fungi offering:

  • specimen data and images
  • species descriptions *
  • county distribution maps *
  • photo galleries *

* Features in production

Currently the site contains data for 90,000 plant specimens from three institutions with rich Chicago Region collections: the Field Museum of Natural History, The Morton Arboretum, and the Chicago Botanic Garden. Species descriptions and fungus specimens will be added in fall 2005.

Specimen data from additional institutions such as the Illinois Natural History Survey and Illinois State Museum will be added by the end of 2006. We have also been in contact with other herbaria in the Great Lakes area such as the University of Wisconsin and University of Michigan regarding the possibility of adding or linking their Chicago Region specimen data through vPlants. This project is a first step towards building a larger online database for plants and fungi that occur in the Great Lakes area and beyond.
